Website about scams

For a school project focused on educating others about avoiding scams, I embarked on the task of creating a comprehensive website. Before diving into coding the website, I employed Adobe XD to craft a visually appealing and user-friendly prototype. This software allowed me to design the website's layout, structure, and interactive elements, ensuring a seamless user experience. By utilizing Adobe XD, I could fine-tune the website's aesthetics and functionality before proceeding to the coding phase, saving time and effort.

To effectively communicate the purpose and features of the website, I also created a compelling video. In this video, I passionately explained the website's core concept, emphasizing the importance of scam prevention and how the platform aimed to empower users with valuable knowledge. By incorporating engaging visuals, clear explanations, and real-life examples, I sought to capture the attention of the audience and inspire them to take proactive measures against scams. The video served as a powerful introduction to the website, generating excitement and anticipation among potential users.

With the website prototype and explanatory video in hand, I transitioned to the coding phase. Armed with my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I brought the prototype to life, ensuring that the final website aligned with the envisioned design. The interactive elements, intuitive navigation, and informative content seamlessly integrated to create an engaging and informative user experience. By combining the technical skills acquired through coding with the creative design process facilitated by Adobe XD, I successfully developed a website that not only educated users about scam prevention but also provided them with practical tools and resources to safeguard themselves in the digital world.
